# Strip Club Map Design Document

## Map Overview
A multiplayer map for ET Legacy with a strip club theme. Allies defend the club's key areas while Axis attempt to capture them.

## Map Layout
- **Main Floor** - Central area with stage, bar, and tables
- **VIP Lounge** - Exclusive area with private dance areas
- **Backstage** - Performers' preparation area
- **Office** - Management office with safe
- **Rooftop** - Exterior area with HVAC systems
- **Parking Lot** - Exterior spawn area for attackers

## Objectives

### Primary Objective
- The safe in the office (Axis must crack it / Allies must defend it)

### Secondary Objectives
- Capture/defend the main stage area
- Secure/protect the VIP lounge
- Construct/prevent a side entrance through the wall
- Establish/disable command posts

## Team Spawns
- **Allies initial spawn**: Backstage area
- **Axis initial spawn**: Parking lot
- **Capturable spawn points**: VIP lounge, main bar area

## Gameplay Flow
1. Allies start with control of the entire strip club facility
2. Axis begin in the parking lot and must breach the club
3. Axis can capture the main stage and VIP lounge to establish forward spawn points
4. Allies must defend these key areas to maintain tactical advantage
5. Axis must construct a side entrance to create an alternative attack route
6. The primary objective is the safe in the office - Axis must crack it while Allies defend
7. Command posts can be established by either team for additional spawn options
8. Round ends when either the safe is cracked (Axis win) or time expires (Allies win)

## Map Features
- Dynamic lighting effects on the main stage
- Interactive elements like dancer poles and bar furniture
- Multiple entry points and tactical positions
- Varied combat environments from close quarters to medium range
- Adult-themed visuals and atmosphere
